Why Browser Tracking is Losing Ground

Your website visitors use ad blockers, privacy browsers, and iOS devices. You're losing visibility into 25–40% of your traffic.

The reality: Browser-based analytics have blind spots you can't see in your reports.

Server-Side Tracking Defined

Instead of collecting data from your visitors' browsers, your server logs what happens and sends it directly to your analytics platform.

It's more reliable, harder to block, and more secure.
